04

Safety protocol

Beauty begins with safety.

In a hospital dedicated exclusively to plastic surgery, no emergencies or infectious diseases circulate. The entire environment — team, workflow and sterilization — exists for planned elective surgery.

  • Dedicated environment

    No emergency room and no infectious-disease admissions: the hospital's flow is 100% elective surgery.

  • In-house sterilization

    Central sterile supply department inside the hospital itself, under the team's direct control.

  • Preoperative planning

    Careful evaluation, testing and individual preparation before any surgical decision.

Studies published in the medical literature associate surgical centers dedicated to a single specialty with lower infection rates compared with multispecialty environments.

Mitchell et al., Journal of Orthopaedics (2013) · Hospital for Special Surgery (2019)

Every surgical procedure involves risks. An individual consultation with a plastic surgeon is essential to determine the appropriate treatment for each case.
