Manifesto

Instead of sharing a practice, they built a hospital.

Two brothers, the same medical training, the same specialty — and a productive disagreement about almost everything else. That combination is what shaped the medical team at Espaço da Plástica: every case passes through two trained sets of eyes that hold each other accountable, the way only brothers do.

At the operating table, trust is not talk: it is procedure. That is why the Anache Anbar brothers took their choice of plastic surgery to its logical conclusion — an entire hospital dedicated to a single specialty, where the team, the operating room and recovery all exist for the same craft.

“There is no transformation without learning and knowledge.”
Motto of the Anache Anbar brothers

  • Board-certified specialists

    Plastic surgeons holding the RQE — Brazil's Specialist Qualification Registry — and members of the Brazilian Society of Plastic Surgery (SBCP).

  • A decade of practice

    More than ten years of surgical practice each, on a path that began in Campo Grande in 2011.

  • A hospital of their own

    Founding partners and administrators of Hospital Espaço da Plástica, in Jardim dos Estados.

  • Teaching

    Professors and speakers — the hospital also hosts hands-on training for physicians, in small groups.
