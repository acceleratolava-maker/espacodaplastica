01

Reception

Arrival is by appointment — and unhurried.

There is no emergency room here. No urgent cases crossing the hallways, no anxious waiting. Everyone who arrives, arrives for a scheduled conversation.

The reception was designed for the interval between the street and the consultation room: serene light, generous seating, discretion. The very first space tells you what this house is here for.

02

Surgical center

The heart of the house beats at an elective pace.

The surgical center is our own and sits inside the hospital — designed for plastic surgery, and nothing else.

  • Dedicated surgical suite

    No transfers between facilities, no rooms shared with other specialties: the entire flow happens under one roof.

  • On-site anesthesia recovery

    After surgery, patients remain in the hospital until the effects of anesthesia wear off and vital signs are stable.

  • A team that lives plastic surgery

    From the operating room to nursing, an entire routine dedicated to planned elective surgery.

03

Procedure rooms

Between the consultation room and the operating room, the right middle ground.

Aesthetic and minimally invasive procedures call for a space of their own — neither a simple office nor a full surgical center.

These rooms are where the hospital's non-surgical treatments take place, with the care and asepsis of a team that also operates. Every procedure, in the setting your medical indication calls for.

04

Recovery

Rooms for the days that matter most.

After the operating room, rest: private rooms where patients remain in the care of our team, with the serenity of not having to go anywhere at all.

Recovery here has a name of its own — hospitality — and an entire page dedicated to it.

05

Central sterilization

Behind the scenes is ours, too.

The central sterile supply department sits inside the hospital itself, under the team's direct control — from preparation to post-use.

In a flow 100% dedicated to planned elective surgery, every instrument has a known origin, process and destination. It's the kind of space patients never visit — and it exists entirely because of them.

06

Technology

Tools in the service of judgment.

Technology here is not a showcase: each device exists to serve a precise indication, defined in a medical evaluation.

  • Morpheus8

    Microneedling combined with radiofrequency for skin treatments of the face and body.

  • CO2 Laser

    Concentrated beams of light used in skin rejuvenation treatments.

  • BodyTite

    Bipolar radiofrequency applied in body contouring procedures.

The same facility also hosts hands-on courses for physicians — a hospital that treats and teaches.
